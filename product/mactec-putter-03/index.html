Features

  • Four popular head designs to suit the eye of all golfers
  • ARC Tech face milling provides improved roll and distance control
  • Alignment aids for easy ball alignment
  • Available with either a premium traditional grip or thicker 3.0 parallel grip
  • Putters available: 001, 002, 003, 004 MRH

Available in four popular head shape designs, the MACTEC putters each beneﬁt from MacGregor’s ARC Tech face milling for improved roll and distance control. Available in a variety of styles from traditional blade to high-MOI mallet, each putter features alignment markers for easy ball alignment and a choice of either traditional or thicker 3.0 parallel grip.
