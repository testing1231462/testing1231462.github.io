This policy sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us and also how you may use our website and information.  Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

If you become a customer then you will need to agree to separate terms and conditions of purchase and you will need to create an account on our website.  Your privacy and data protection rights as a customer will be dealt with in those separate terms.

Under data protection legislation the data controller is Worldwide Golf Brands LTD

Information we may collect from you

If you are a casual visitor to our website then we will not collect any personal information about you except for temporary use of cookies or other identifiers which are needed for the proper functioning of our website.

However, in time we may add features and services to our website in which case we may collect and process the following data about you (but only with your consent):

  • Information that you provide by filling in forms on our website http://www.stuburt.com/ & http://www.macgregor-golf.co.uk/ (“our website”). This includes information provided by you when you create an account on our website, subscribing to our services, posting material or requesting further services and/or information. As our website evolves we may add these additional features. We may also ask for information when you report any problems.
  • If you contact us, we may keep a record of any correspondence.
  • We may also ask you to complete surveys that we use for research purposes, although you do not have to respond to them.
  • Details of transactions you carry out through our site and of the fulfilment of your orders (please see our separate terms of purchase).
  • Details of your visits to our site including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data, whether this is required for our own billing purposes (if relevant) and the resources that you access.
  • Where you look for the nearest store then we will use a third-party service to try and identify your location based on your IP address.

IP addresses

We may collect information about your computer, including where available your IP address, operating system and browser type, for system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers. This is statistical data about our users’ browsing actions and patterns and does not identify any individual.

If you have logged into our website and our using our online shop then we will log this information against your user ID for security purposes and to track any orders.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site. We use cookies for:

  • identifying you and tracking your preferences during that session,
  • noting the last selection of products on the product grid,

Where you have set your preferences in your browser for permitting or disallowing “cookies” then we will endeavour to honour that selection apart from where this would make our website inoperable and they are temporary.

Where we store your personal data

The data that we collect from you will only be stored and processed in the European Economic Area (“EEA”).

If you place an order with us then we will provide your personal details, including your name, address and payment/credit card information to our payment providers for the processing of any payments.  Similarly, your contact details will be provided to our courier company for delivery purposes.

By submitting an order, you agree to this.  We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable privacy and data protection laws.

All information you provide to us is stored on our secure servers.  Any payment transactions will be encrypted [using SSL technology.  Where we have given you (or where you have chosen) a password which enables you to access certain parts of our site, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential.  You may not share that password with anyone.

Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the Internet is not completely secure.  Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee the security of your data en route to us.  Once we have received your information, we will use strict procedures and security features to guard against unauthorised access.

Please see the third-party advertisers section below.
